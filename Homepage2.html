About Us

Shibuya Solutions Inc. is an information Technology Company that is dedicated to providing white labe, fully customizable digital ticketing and service voucher solutions capable of processing digital service coupons and traceable vouchers for service-oriented commercial transactions. Shibuya aims to provide its clients with competitive advantages, using solutions and strategic social marketing to help build a solid customer base.
